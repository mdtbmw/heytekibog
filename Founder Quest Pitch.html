Founder's Quest™ Game Guide

Welcome to Founder's Quest™, an interactive, AI-powered business simulation game meticulously crafted by Daniel Innocent (@MDTBMW) of HeyTek Limited. This game is designed to immerse you in the entrepreneurial journey, transforming complex startup development into an engaging, educational, and ultimately rewarding experience.

Your mission is to navigate the challenges of building a startup from a raw idea to a successful launch. You'll be guided by the proprietary iBOG™ Framework, making critical decisions, managing resources, and experiencing the direct consequences of your strategic choices.

This guide will walk you through each phase of the game, explaining its objectives, key activities, and how to interact with the game's AI mentors and systems.

1. The iBOG™ Framework

The entire game is structured around HeyTek's iBOG™ Framework, a practical, stage-based methodology for startup development:

  • Ideate: Clarify, refine, and validate your business idea.
  • Build: Construct the foundational elements of your venture – brand, team, and initial offerings.
  • Operate: (Currently Simulated) SIMULATED Manage daily operations, navigate challenges, and optimize for efficiency.
  • Grow: (Currently Simulated) SIMULATED Implement growth strategies, explore market expansion, and prepare for launch.

Successfully navigating each phase will improve your startup's core metrics and bring you closer to a successful launch.

2. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) / Scores

Your progress and the health of your startup are measured by four key scores, visible on your main dashboard:

  • Clarity Score: Reflects how well-defined, validated, and understood your core business concept, mission, and vision are. Activities in the IDEATE phase heavily influence this.
  • Structure Score: Measures the solidity of your operational foundation, including your brand identity, team composition, and defined workflows. Influenced primarily by the BUILD phase.
  • Execution Power: Represents your ability to manage tasks, run the business effectively, and handle operational challenges. This score is developed in the OPERATE phase.
  • Market Reception: Indicates how well your product/service is being received by your target audience and your success in scaling. This is primarily built in the GROW phase.

Each score is crucial, and maintaining a balance is key to overall success. These scores contribute to your "Total Impact" at the end of the game.

3. TekCoins (TC)

TekCoins are the in-game currency of TekVille. You start with an initial balance (e.g., 1000 TC). TekCoins are used for:

  • Consulting with in-game experts like Daniel Innocent.
  • Utilizing specific TekerBot AI services (e.g., advanced idea generation, chat).
  • Potentially other strategic investments or costs as the game evolves.

Managing your TekCoins wisely is essential for accessing valuable resources and support throughout your journey.

4. TekerBot - Your AI Mentor

TekerBot is your primary AI guide and mentor throughout Founder's Quest™. TekerBot will:

  • Provide contextual advice and encouragement at the start of each phase and activity.
  • Help generate raw business ideas if you choose.
  • Facilitate the Validation Gauntlet by simulating stakeholder interactions.
  • Offer an "Idea Incubation" program if your idea is flagged during validation.
  • Be available for chat (costing TekCoins) to answer questions about your current phase, the iBOG framework, or general startup advice.
  • Assist with AI-powered content generation for elements like Mission Statements, Vision Statements, and Taglines (costing TekCoins).

Pay attention to TekerBot's insights; they are designed to help you succeed!

5. Daniel Innocent - Expert Consultant

Daniel Innocent, the Brand & Operations Strategist from HeyTek Limited, is an in-game expert you can consult. His services provide significant boosts to your startup but require an investment of TekCoins. Available consultations include:

  • Lean Canvas Strategy Session: Refines your Lean Canvas for maximum clarity.
  • Mission & Brand Resonance Workshop: Helps craft a compelling MVV.
  • Brand Identity Kickstart: Develops a foundational brand identity.
  • Core Team Synergy Analysis: Optimizes your team structure.

Consulting Daniel can fast-track certain activities and significantly improve your scores.

The IDEATE phase is all about laying a strong conceptual foundation for your venture. The goal is to achieve a high Clarity Score by defining and validating your business idea.

1.1 Lean Canvas: Strategic Blueprint

  • Objective: To map out the core components of your business model on a one-page strategic diagram.
  • How it Works: You'll be presented with "Insight Orbs" representing different business aspects (e.g., Pain Point Focus, Feature X, Subscription Model). Drag these orbs to the corresponding sections of the Lean Canvas (Problem, Solution, Unique Value Proposition, Unfair Advantage, Channels, Customer Segments, Key Metrics, Cost Structure, Revenue Streams).
  • Completion: The activity is considered complete once you've mapped at least three orbs. Submitting your canvas finalizes this step.
  • Consultancy: Daniel Innocent can help refine your Lean Canvas for 150 TC, auto-completing it with expert insights.

1.2 Define: Mission, Vision & Values (MVV)

  • Objective: To articulate the guiding principles of your company.
  • How it Works:
    • Write a Mission Statement (your "Why").
    • Write a Vision Statement (your "Where").
    • Select 3-5 Core Values (your "How") from a provided list (e.g., Innovation, Integrity, Customer-Centricity).
  • AI Assistance: TekerBot can help generate your Mission and Vision statements for a TekCoin fee.
  • Completion: This activity is complete once all fields are filled and submitted.
  • Consultancy: Daniel Innocent can help craft your MVV for 200 TC.

1.3 Validation Gauntlet: The Market's Verdict

  • Objective: To test the viability of your idea by "pitching" it to a simulated stakeholder (customer, investor, or critic) controlled by TekerBot.
  • How it Works: You'll engage in a text-based (with optional voice input) conversation. Your responses will directly affect your Validation Score (starts at 50%).
  • Success/Failure:
    • If your Validation Score reaches 100% (or stays high after several turns), you pass validation.
    • If your Validation Score drops to 0%, your idea is flagged.
  • Idea Flagged & TekerBot Incubation: If your idea is flagged, you cannot proceed directly. An "Idea Flagged!" modal will appear, prompting you to engage TekerBot for an "Idea Incubation" program. This costs 75 TC and involves:
    • TekerBot analyzing your failed validation chat.
    • Offering practice questions or allowing you to choose your next stakeholder type.
    • Once incubation is complete (or you choose a new stakeholder), you re-enter the Validation Gauntlet with a reset score and a new (or similar) stakeholder.
  • Anti-Cheat: Switching tabs or suspicious input during active validation may penalize your Validation Score.
  • Completion: This activity is marked complete and confirmed only after successfully passing the Validation Gauntlet.

To complete the IDEATE phase and proceed to BUILD, all activities (Lean Canvas, MVV, Validation Gauntlet) must be confirmed, and your Clarity Score must be at least 30%.

With a validated idea, the BUILD phase focuses on creating the tangible assets and structures of your company. The goal is to achieve a strong Structure Score.

2.1 Branding Suite: Forge Your Identity

  • Objective: To define your company's visual and verbal identity.
  • How it Works:
    • Select a Logo Archetype (e.g., Modern, Classic, Playful, Minimalist).
    • Craft a Tagline (AI assistance available from TekerBot for TC).
    • Choose a Brand Color Palette (Primary and Secondary colors).
    • Select a Brand Typography style.
    • Select 3-5 Key Product Features from a list.
  • Completion: Finalize your choices to complete this activity.
  • Consultancy: Daniel Innocent can provide a Brand Identity Kickstart for 300 TC.

2.2 Assemble Core Team: The Architects of Your Vision

  • Objective: To hire key leadership roles for your startup.
  • How it Works: Drag and drop talent from TekVille's "Available Talent Pool" into the mandatory roles of CEO, CTO, and CMO. An optional COO role is also available. Each character has specific skills and a tooltip describing their strengths.
  • Skill Match: The game will indicate the skill match (Good, Okay, Poor) for each assignment.
  • Completion: Confirm your team once all mandatory roles are filled.
  • Consultancy: Daniel Innocent offers a Core Team Synergy Analysis for 250 TC.

2.3 Define Core Workflow: Operational Blueprint SIMULATED

  • Objective: To outline the key steps in your product/service delivery.
  • How it Works: This activity is currently simulated. Clicking "Simulate Workflow Definition" will mark it as complete and provide a score boost. Full interactive workflow mapping is planned for future updates.

2.4 Draft Offer Page: Your Digital Handshake SIMULATED

  • Objective: To create a compelling description of your core offer.
  • How it Works: This activity is also currently simulated. Clicking "Simulate Offer Page Draft" completes it and boosts scores. A full interactive page builder is a future feature.

To complete the BUILD phase and proceed to OPERATE, all activities must be confirmed, and your Structure Score must be at least 25%.

The OPERATE phase is where you manage the day-to-day running of your business. The primary goal is to build your Execution Power Score.

3.1 Daily Operations: The Grind & The Glory (Simulated)

  • Objective: To simulate managing daily tasks, responding to market changes, handling crises, and keeping your team motivated.
  • How it Works: This entire phase is currently simulated. Clicking "Simulate Operations Cycle" will complete the activity, affect your Execution Score, and potentially your TekCoins balance.
  • Future: Full interactive operational challenges, decision-making scenarios, and resource management are planned for future updates.

To complete the OPERATE phase and proceed to GROW, this simulated activity must be confirmed, and your Execution Power must be at least 25%.

In the GROW phase, your focus shifts to scaling your venture and expanding your market reach. The aim is to improve your Market Reception Score.

4.1 Market Expansion: Reach New Horizons (Simulated)

  • Objective: To simulate launching marketing campaigns, exploring new customer segments or geographical markets, and optimizing operations for growth.
  • How it Works: This phase is currently simulated. Clicking "Simulate Growth Initiatives" completes the activity, impacting your Market Reception Score and TekCoins.
  • Future: Full interactive growth strategy challenges, marketing campaign management, and simulated investor pitches are planned.

To complete the GROW phase and LAUNCH your company, this simulated activity must be confirmed, and your Market Reception Score must be at least 25%.

Congratulations, Founder! Completing all four phases of the iBOG™ framework means you have successfully launched your startup in TekVille.

  • Final Scores: Your dashboard will display your final scores for Clarity, Structure, Execution, and Market Reception, along with your Total Impact score and remaining TekCoins.

This document (simulated as a text/JSON output in the current version) summarizes all the key decisions, strategies, and definitions you made throughout your Founder's Quest™ journey (e.g., company name, idea, Lean Canvas details, MVV, branding choices, team).
  • Play Again: You can choose to restart the game and build a new legacy with a different idea or strategy.
